Data

We intend to optimize the procedures and processes involved with the deposition and access to human datasets. These procedures should be simplified from a technical and organisational perspective while ensuring full compliance to data protection and safety standards.

To this aim, a diverse and distributed data infrastructure, involving major data generators and using scalable cloud technologies, will have to be integrated and exposed to researchers via a simple data access portal. Additionally, the communication with data access committees and the technical details of regulating distributed data access need to be streamlined.
