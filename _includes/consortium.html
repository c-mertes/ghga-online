Security

The GHGA is designed to provide an appropriate archive for data on subjects who have consented to the use of their individual genetic data for biomedical research, but not for unlimited public data release.

Flexibility

Data can be submitted to the GHGA prior to publication, at other significant milestones, and at study close in accordance with the Toronto statement.
